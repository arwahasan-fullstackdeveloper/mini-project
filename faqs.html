Frequently Asked Questions

The best way to store fruit depends on the type. Generally, keep fruit in a cool, dry place. Some fruits, like berries, should be refrigerated, while others, like bananas, do better at room temperature.

Ripe fruit usually has a pleasant aroma and yields slightly to gentle pressure. Color can also indicate ripeness, but it varies between fruit types.

Organic fruits are grown without synthetic pesticides and fertilizers. Some people prefer organic due to environmental or health reasons, but both organic and non-organic fruits can be nutritious.
